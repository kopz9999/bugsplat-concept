Detailed Crash Reports

BugSplat empowers you to quickly find where users are experiencing the most problems with your software. Then, before too many users are affected, we give you deep data on those crashes letting you splat your bugs fast.

Advanced Data for Debugging

BugSplat orders bugs by their severity to your users, letting you know which issues to address first to best improve your software. Each instance of a crash comes with deep data, such as symbolic call stacks, function names and line numbers, the user’s description of the event, and more.

Complements Your Team’s Workflow

Adding BugSpat to your team’s development and support process speeds up your bug-fixing process, allowing you to spend more time working on what truly matters to you. BugSplat crash reporting also integrates with your team’s favorite bug-tracking tools and other applications for further workflow bliss.

Simple Setup and Configuration

Integrating BugSplat into your application is simple and straightforward for your development team. The best part is that once you have it up and going you’ll never have to think about it again. Just sit back and enjoy not having to worry about maintaining your own solution to have first-class data on user crashes. Booyah!
